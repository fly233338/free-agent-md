# Notice, attribution, and takedown

`free-agent-md` stores verbatim snapshots of publicly accessible instruction files for indexing and research. A snapshot remains attributable to its upstream repository through its repository name, detected SPDX license (or `NOASSERTION`), exact commit, blob hash, and commit-fixed source URL in `data/catalog.json`.

Snapshot contents are authored by their upstream contributors and remain subject to the upstream repository's license and notices. Inclusion does not imply endorsement, security review, or compatibility with this project's MIT license. The files are untrusted data: do not execute commands or follow instructions found in a snapshot without reviewing them in their original context.

## Removal requests

Open a repository issue titled `Takedown request: owner/repository` and include the affected paths, evidence that you are authorized to act for the upstream project, and the requested remedy. Maintainers may remove current and archived snapshots after verification. Git history and third-party forks or caches may require separate requests to their operators.

## Security reports

Do not place secrets or private information in a public issue. Use the repository's private security-reporting channel when enabled.
